"Gelo"

Hey, I’m Chris Angelo, a 22-year-old Computer Engineering graduate from Pampanga State University — formerly known as Don Honorio Ventura State University (DHVSU) — based in San Matias, Sta. Rita, Pampanga. I’ve always been drawn to technology — not just how it works, but how it can change the way we live, think, and create. That curiosity naturally led me to software development, where I feel most at home.

I’m most comfortable coding in Python, but I’m always looking to expand my skills. C++ is next on my list since I didn’t get much hands-on experience with it in school, and I’m eager to see what I can build with it. I love challenging myself with new tools and languages — not just to learn, but to solve real problems and bring ideas to life.

Right now, I’m focused on building experience, gaining knowledge, and most importantly, enjoying the journey. I’m not rushing into anything — I want to stay passionate, stay curious, and keep doing work that feels meaningful and exciting.

When I’m not coding or geeking out over tech stuff, I usually spend my time watching random YouTube videos, working out, or just taking a break to clear my head. I try to keep things balanced — learn hard, chill harder.
